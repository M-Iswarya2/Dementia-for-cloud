About DementiaAI

Pioneering AI-powered early detection for cognitive health

Our Mission

DementiaAI is dedicated to revolutionizing early dementia detection through cutting-edge artificial intelligence and accessible screening tools. We believe that early detection can significantly improve outcomes and quality of life for individuals at risk of cognitive decline.

Our mission is to make cognitive health screening as simple and routine as checking your blood pressure, empowering individuals and healthcare providers with actionable insights for proactive care.

Who We Serve

Individuals & Families

Monitor cognitive health from home with easy-to-use screening tools. Perfect for those concerned about memory changes or family history of dementia.

Healthcare Providers

Enhance patient care with AI-assisted screening. Our tools complement clinical assessments and help identify at-risk patients earlier.

Researchers

Access large-scale cognitive health data for studies. Partner with us to advance dementia research and develop better interventions.
